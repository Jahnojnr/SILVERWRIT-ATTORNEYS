Common questions about contacting our legal team

We aim to respond to all consultation requests within 24 hours during business days. For urgent matters marked as "Emergency," we respond within 2 hours or immediately if you call our emergency line at 0803 456 7890.

To help us assist you efficiently, please have the following ready:

  • A clear description of your legal issue

  • Key dates and deadlines
  • Names of other parties involved

Our payment terms vary depending on the type of legal service:

  • Consultation: Free initial consultation

  • Flat Fee: For specific services like contract drafting
  • Contingency: For certain types of litigation (discussed case by case)

All fees are discussed and agreed upon before we begin work.
